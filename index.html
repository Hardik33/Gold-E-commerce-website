Our values

At bee’Dahab, we believe in the power of elegance and the statement that a piece of jewelry can make. Our core values revolve around:

Quality: We provide only the highest quality 21 & 22k gold.

Elegance: Our designs are crafted to embody sophistication and class.

Empowerment: We create jewelry for the modern, boss woman who knows her worth.


Our story

Founded in Nairobi, Kenya, bee’Dahab was born out of a passion for timeless beauty and sophisticated design. Our journey began with a desire to offer women pieces that not only complement their style but also empower them to feel confident and chic.
